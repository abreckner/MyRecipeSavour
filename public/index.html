Getting started

Are you tired of having to print out the recipes you find online in order to save them?

Well, my wife was, so I decided to make it easy for her to save and catalog her recipes

Simply add the URL of the recipe you like, and if the site has been cataloged, we will copy the recipe over into your recipe book.

If your recipe belongs to a site that is not cataloged, you can copy and paste it in for now and we will be alerted to catalog that site as soon as we can

So what are you waiting for? Go to your recipe book.
